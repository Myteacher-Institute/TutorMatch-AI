About Us

A trusted tutor marketplace for Nigerian families

Myteacherconnect helps parents and students instantly connect with verified, professional home tutors. Our mission is to make high-quality, personalized education accessible to every child in Nigeria.

Verified Tutors

Every tutor undergoes rigorous identity checks, academic credential verification, and background screening before matching with students.

Simple AI Discovery

No complex forms or filters. Parents can describe what their child needs in natural language, and our AI matches them with the ideal tutor.

Platform-Managed Payouts

Payments are managed on-platform and tutor payouts are released weekly after student confirmation or support review.
